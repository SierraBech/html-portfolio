The Best Movies According to Sierra!

My Top 3 Movies of All Time.

Emperor`s New Groove

A movie I can watch over and over again and never get tired of it. It`s hilarious and witty and the theme song is such a catch!

Drop Dead Fred

This movie reminds me of my childhood and I love it for that reason. It`s quite a funny movie as well.

Stranger Than Fiction

I love this movie because it`s an awesome idea to bring a book to life and I love all the actors in that movie.
